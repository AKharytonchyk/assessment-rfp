## Notes

1. **Rates are from the EPAM & CAPG Central Europe rate card.** Daily rates assume an 8-hour billable day.
2. **Person-days** are computed as `weeks on project x 5 days/week`.
3. All team members work **Central European business hours** with a minimum 4-hour overlap with US Eastern.
4. The Delivery Manager acts as scrum master and primary client liaison throughout the engagement.
5. The Solution Architect is staffed 100% through Week 14. **Phase 4 architectural coverage (Wk 15–16)** — for DOC-008 migration plan sign-off, operating-model attestation (GOV-006), and SEC-005b classification review — is provided via **50% part-time SA cover** (10 PD, billed at the same Lead rate); this may be the original SA or a substitute. The 10 PD sits inside the existing priced envelope (absorbed by the schedule reserve / rework bucket in the Priced-PD uplift) and does not increase the headline ROM.
6. DE-2 is a tactical ramp-up to accelerate pipeline development during the core build phase (Wk 3-12).
7. The Technical Writer joins in Week 13 to produce runbooks, operating guides, and handover documentation while the platform is feature-complete and stabilizing.
8. **No bench time is billed.** Team members are only invoiced for the weeks they are actively on the project.
9. **Priced PD vs. Estimable PD.** The 500 PD billed here is priced T&M capacity. The WBS (`wbs.md`) captures 293 PD of directly estimable work. The ~1.71× uplift is the fully-loaded overhead — ceremonies, standups, governance, ramp-up, PTO, and a 15% rework reserve — baked into the daily rate, not invoiced separately. Full reconciliation in `wbs.md` §"WBS Estimable PD vs. Priced PD" and `rom.md` §3.
